## Getting Started

### Q1: How do I sign up and create my first group?

**A**: Follow these steps:

1. **Sign Up**
   - Open TillSave app
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Enter your phone number
   - Verify the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your phone
   - Create a 4-digit PIN (remember this!)
   - Enter your name

2. **Create Your First Group**
   - After sign up, click "Create New Group"
   - Enter group name (e.g., "Monday Savings Circle")
   - Set your contribution target (optional)
   - Click "Create Group"
   - You'll get a **unique 6-digit code** (e.g., A1B2C3)

3. **Share the Code**
   - Share this code with your members
   - Members join using: Menu â†’ "Join Group" â†’ Enter code

**Tip**: Write down your code and keep it safe. Members need it to join.

---

### Q2: How do I add members to my group?

**A**: Members add themselves:

1. **Members Use the Code**
   - Share your 6-digit group code with members
   - They open TillSave
   - Go to Menu â†’ "Join Group"
   - Enter your code
   - They're automatically added

2. **You Can See Them**
   - Go to Group Dashboard
   - "Members" tab shows all members
   - See their names and payment status

3. **Invite Members**
   - You can also share the code via WhatsApp, SMS, or in person
   - Members can join anytime during the cycle

**Note**: Members who join mid-cycle will still contribute from that date forward.

---

### Q3: What if a member forgets their PIN?

**A**: 
- They click "Forgot PIN" on login screen
- Verify phone number with OTP
- Create a new PIN
- They're back in!

**You can help them**:
- You don't need their PIN to manage the group
- They need PIN only for their own account
- Encourage them to remember/write it down safely

## Recording Payments

### Q4: How do I record a payment when a member gives me money?

**A**: Simple 3-step process:

1. **Open Your Group**
   - Select your group from dashboard
   - Click "Record Payment" button

2. **Enter Payment Details**
   - **Member Name**: Select from dropdown
   - **Amount**: Type the amount received
   - **Currency**: Choose RWF, USD, EUR, etc.
   - **Date**: Automatically today (can change if needed)
   - **Note**: Optional (e.g., "Cash from METERO")

3. **Confirm**
   - Review the information
   - Click "Record Payment"
   - âœ… Payment saved! Members see it instantly

**Tip**: Record payments daily or after you receive them. This keeps records accurate.

## Payouts & Money

### Q9: How is my commission calculated?

**A**: You earn **1 day's contribution per member**

**Formula**:
```
Your Commission = Member's Daily Rate Ã— Number of Members

Example:
- 5 members
- Each member contributes 5,000 RWF/day
- Your commission = 5,000 Ã— 5 = 25,000 RWF per cycle
```

**Why?**
- You spend time managing the group
- You handle money and trust
- You organize payouts
- Your commission is fair compensation

**Member Gets**:
```
Member Payout = (Total Saved) - (Your Commission)

Example:
- Member saved 150,000 RWF
- Your commission is 5,000 RWF (1 day)
- Member gets: 150,000 - 5,000 = 145,000 RWF
```

### Q12: What if a member leaves mid-cycle?

**A**: They keep what they saved:

**Example**:
- Member was in for 15 days
- Saved 5,000 RWF/day = 75,000 total
- Cycle continues for other members
- When cycle ends, they get their 75,000 (minus commission)
- They're not in next cycle unless they rejoin

**To Handle**:
1. Stop recording their payments
2. At payout time, they still get their saved amount
3. They can rejoin in next cycle if they want

## Multi-Currency Support

### Q13: How do I handle multiple currencies?

**A**: TillSave tracks each currency separately:

**Recording Payments**:
1. Select member
2. Enter amount
3. **Choose currency**: RWF, USD, EUR, KES, etc.
4. Save

**Dashboard Shows**:
```
Total Saved:
- 2,500,000 RWF
- 1,500 USD
- 500 EUR

Your Earnings:
- 150,000 RWF
- 100 USD
- 30 EUR
```

**Payouts Are Separate**:
- Member A gets 150,000 RWF + 100 USD
- Member B gets 180,000 RWF only
- Each member gets back what they contributed

**No Conversion**:
- We don't convert currencies
- Keep it simple: each member gets back their currency
- You handle exchange if needed offline

---

**On Payout**:

Member A (Rwanda):

Member B (USA):

Member C (UK):

**You Handle Transfer**:
- You move money to each member's location

## Technical Issues

### Q17: What if the app crashes or I lose internet?

**A**: TillSave is built for offline:

**Offline Mode**:
- App works without internet
- You can view groups, members, payments
- Recording payments: Works offline
- Data saved locally on your phone

**When Internet Returns**:
- App syncs automatically
- Everything uploads
- No data loss

**Best Practice**:
- Use app however you want (online or offline)
- Check internet connection periodically
- Data is always safe
