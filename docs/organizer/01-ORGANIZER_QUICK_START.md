**Get up and running in 5 minutes!**

## Step 1: Download the App (1 minute)

1. Open **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android)
2. Search: `TillSave`
3. Tap **Install**
4. Wait for download

---

## Step 2: Sign Up (2 minutes)

1. Open TillSave app
2. Tap **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your **phone number**
4. Check SMS for **OTP** (One-Time Password)
5. Enter the 4-digit code
6. Create your **PIN** (write it down somewhere safe!)
7. Enter your **name**

When a member gives you money:

1. Open TillSave
2. Select your group
3. Tap **"Record Payment"**
4. Select **member name**
5. Type **amount** received
6. Choose **currency** (RWF/USD/EUR/etc.)
7. Confirm **date**
8. Tap **"Record Payment"**

## What Happens at End of Cycle

After 30 days:

1. **Review**: App shows breakdown of who paid what
2. **Confirm**: Click "Finalize Payout" with new confirmation modal
3. **Print**: Save payout report (PDF)
4. **Pay**: Distribute money to members
5. **Restart**: Click "Start Cycle 2" - immediate restart!
