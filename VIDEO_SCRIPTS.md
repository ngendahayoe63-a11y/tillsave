## VIDEO 1: "How to Record a Payment" (3 minutes)

**Goal**: Teach organizers the most important daily task

**Equipment Needed**:
- Phone with TillSave app
- Ring light or good lighting (optional)
- Quiet room
- Phone mounted on stand or in hand

---

**[INTRO - 0:00]**

> "Hi! I'm [YOUR_NAME] from TillSave. 
>

>

**[OPEN APP - 0:15]**

> "First, open the TillSave app.
> 
> [Show phone screen]
> 
> Select your group - I'm going to select 'Monday Savings Circle'.
> 
> [Tap on group]
> 
> Now click the green 'Record Payment' button."

**[RECORD PAYMENT FORM - 0:30]**

> "The form appears. Here's what you need:
> 
> **First**: Select the member name from the dropdown. 
> [Tap dropdown, show options]
> I'm going to select 'METERO Aloys'.
> 
> **Second**: Type the amount they gave you.
> [Type amount: 5000]
> Let's say he gave me 5,000 RWF.
> 
> **Third**: Choose the currency.
> [Tap currency, show RWF/USD/EUR options]
> We're using RWF, so I select that.
> 
> **Fourth**: The date. It's automatically today, which is correct.
> If they paid you yesterday, you can change it.
> 
> **Fifth** (optional): Add a note. 
> Like 'Cash from METERO' or 'Mobile payment'. 
> This helps you remember if someone paid twice."

**[SAVE - 1:45]**

> "Now I click 'Record Payment'.
> 
> [Click button]

> ‚úÖ Done! The payment appears in the list immediately.

**[DASHBOARD UPDATE - 2:00]**

> "Notice the dashboard updated automatically:
> - Total saved went up
> - METERO's name shows as contributed
> - Everything is live, everyone can see it

> [Swipe to show dashboard changes]"

**[MULTIPLE CURRENCIES - 2:15]**

> "Quick note: If someone pays you in dollars or euros, just select that currency instead of RWF.

> [Show currency dropdown again]

---

### Camera Directions

1. **Opening**: Show your face (build trust)
2. **Form filling**: Zoom into phone screen (so they see what they're tapping)
3. **Dashboard**: Show the real update (prove it works)
4. **Pace**: Slow enough to follow, fast enough not to bore
5. **Lighting**: Phone screen should be visible and bright
6. **Sound**: Quiet background, speak clearly

### Recording Tips

- Record in one take (don't worry about perfection)
- If you mess up a word, just keep going
- Zoom in on phone screen when showing buttons/numbers
- Pause 2 seconds after each step for people to absorb
- Smile at beginning and end

## VIDEO 2: "Understanding Your Payout Report" (3 minutes)

**Goal**: Help organizers understand the final number and trust the math

**Equipment Needed**:
- Phone with TillSave app (or example printed report)
- Report document (printed or on screen)
- Ring light (optional)
- Quiet room

---

### Script

**[INTRO - 0:00]**

> "Hi! I'm [YOUR_NAME] from TillSave.
> 
> By the end of a 30-day cycle, you'll have a payout report.
> This video explains what all those numbers mean.
> 
> It's simpler than it looks."

**[SHOW REPORT - 0:15]**

> 
> [Hold up printed report or show on screen]

> **Dates**: Shows when the cycle ran (Dec 1 - Dec 30)

> This is how much money all members will get back.
> NOT including your commission.
>

> You earn 1 day per member = 5 members √ó 5,000 RWF = 25,000 RWF... 
> wait, that doesn't match?
> 
> [If needed, explain this is because members contributed different amounts]
>

**[SECTION 3: MEMBER TABLE - 1:45]**

> "Here's the main table with each member:
> 
> **Name**: The member's name
> **Days Paid**: How many days they contributed (some paid all 30, some paid 20)

>

>

> 
> This prevents mistakes."

**[SIGNATURE & DISTRIBUTION - 2:45]**

> "At the bottom is a signature line.

> It proves they received it.
>

**[OUTRO - 2:55]**

**[END - 3:00]**

---

### Camera Directions

1. **Opening**: Show your face (build credibility)
2. **Report**: Hold printed report up and zoom to numbers
3. **Close-ups**: Show member table clearly
4. **Pace**: Slower than Video 1 (more numbers to absorb)
5. **Pointing**: Use finger to point at specific numbers
6. **Expression**: Confident (make them trust the math)

- Have the printed report in front of you while recording
- Point to each number as you explain it
- Don't worry about explaining the complete math formula

- End with confidence that the app does this right

## How to Record These Videos

### Simple Setup
1. **Phone on stand** (use a book or tripod)
2. **Good lighting** (natural window is best)
3. **Quiet room** (no background noise)
4. **Tap to record** (use default phone camera app)

### Recording Process
1. Open Notes app with script
2. Position phone to show your face + screen
3. Press record
4. Read script naturally (not robotic!)
5. Show app on screen when talking about buttons
6. Stop and save when done

### Editing (Optional)
- You can edit on phone with iMovie (iOS) or CapCut (Android)
- Or keep raw recording (people prefer authentic)
- Add title slide at start: "[Your Name] - TillSave Tutorial"

### Uploading
- Save to Google Drive, YouTube, or Dropbox
- Share link with organizers via email
- Include in welcome email
